Doro Wat

Doro Wot is a dish we always make for celebrations, like birthdays or holidays. My mom says, "Doro Wot takes time, but it’s worth it when the whole family sits down to eat."It’s one of those meals that reminds me how special family traditions can be.

Tips and Tricks

  • Marinate the chicken with lemon juice for added flavor.
  • Slow-cook the onions until caramelized for depth of flavor.
  • Serve with boiled eggs for authenticity.

Recipe

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken, cut into pieces 1 whole
Onions, finely chopped 4 cups
Berbere spice 3 tbsp
Garlic, minced 5 cloves
Ginger, minced 1 tbsp

instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with lemon juice and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the onions on low heat until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and berbere, cooking until fragrant.
  4. Add the chicken and simmer for 45 minutes.

Prep Time: 1 hour | Servings: 6
