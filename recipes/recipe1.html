Tibs

Tibs is one of my family’s favorite dishes. We make it whenever we have guests over or just want something special for dinner. My dad always says, "Tibs is best when everyone’s in the kitchen helping out" It’s a dish that brings us all together.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use fresh garlic and ginger for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the spice level by adding more or less berbere.
  • Serve hot with injera or rice for the best experience.

Recipe

Ingredient Quantity
Beef, cubed 1 lb
Onions, chopped 1 cup
Garlic, minced 3 cloves
Berbere spice 2 tbsp
Salt To taste

instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the beef until browned.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and berbere spice, stirring well.
  3. Cook for 10-15 minutes until the meat is tender and flavors meld together.

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Servings: 4
